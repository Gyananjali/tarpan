Privacy Policy for Tarpan

Effective Date: 08 Jun 2026
App Name: Tarpan
Developer / Legal Entity: Gyananjali

Introduction

Tarpan is a Hindu spiritual and religious utility application designed to assist users in performing Pitru Tarpan and related ancestral rituals. The application provides ritual guidance, mantras, procedures, and related spiritual content to help users perform ancestral offerings and observances.

1. Information We Collect

To provide certain features of the application, Tarpan may collect or store limited information:

  • Google account information when users sign in using Google Sign-In (such as name, email address, and profile information made available by Google).
  • User-entered ritual information, including names of ancestors, family members, relatives, Gotra, and other details required for performing Tarpan rituals.
  • Basic application preferences and settings.

2. How Information Is Used

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Authenticating users through Google Sign-In.
  • Personalizing ritual content and Tarpan procedures.
  • Saving user-entered ancestral and family details for future use.
  • Improving user experience and application functionality.

3. Local Processing and Offline Functionality

Tarpan is primarily an offline application. Ritual calculations, content generation, and most application functions are processed locally on the user's device.

User-entered ritual information is generally stored locally on the device. In cases where backup, synchronization, or account-linked features are provided, selected information may be stored securely in application databases associated with the user's account.

4. Data Sharing

Tarpan does not sell, rent, or trade user information.

User information is not shared with third parties except:

  • When required for Google authentication services.
  • When required by applicable law, legal process, or governmental request.
  • When necessary to protect the rights, safety, or security of users or the application.

5. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Children's Privacy

The application is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental involvement.

7. Third-Party Services

The application may use services provided by Google, including Google Sign-In, which are governed by Google's own privacy policies and terms of service.

8. Data Retention

User information remains stored for as long as it is required to provide application functionality or until the user removes the information, deletes the account, or requests deletion where applicable.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the application after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
