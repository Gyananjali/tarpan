Tarpan Account and Data Deletion

Tarpan users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data at any time.

To request account deletion, send an email to:

devayushgoswami@gmail.com

Information to Include

Please include the following information in your email:

  • The email address used to sign in to Tarpan
  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Any additional details that may help us identify your account

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon successful verification and processing of your request, we will delete:

  • Your user profile information
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Personal information associated with your account
  • Application data linked to your account

Data Retention

Some information may be retained for a limited period where required for:

  • Legal and regulatory compliance
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • System backup and recovery processes

Any retained data will be securely handled and deleted when no longer required.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are generally processed within 30 days of verification.

Contact

If you have questions about your personal data or this deletion process, please contact:

devayushgoswami@gmail.com
